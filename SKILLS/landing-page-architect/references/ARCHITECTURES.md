# Landing Page Architecture Reference

Detailed breakdown and examples for each of the 8 sections of a high-converting landing page.

## 1. HERO
The goal is to stop the scroll and filter for the right audience.
- **Eyebrow**: 3-5 words identifying the audience (e.g., "For SaaS Founders Ready to Scale")
- **Headline**: Big, bold promise or outcome.
- **Subheadline**: How you achieve the promise + objection handling.
- **CTA**: Clear action (e.g., "Get the Free Guide").
- **Trust Signals**: Logos, "Used by 500+ teams", etc.

## 2. SUCCESS (Conditional)
Used for confirmation pages or lead magnet delivery.
- **Checkmark**: Visual confirmation of success.
- **Confirmation**: "You're in! Check your inbox."
- **Deliverable list**: What they just got or what happens next.

Emphasize the pain points to build desire for a solution.
- **3 Problems**: Be specific. "Losing $1k/mo because of X."
- **Agitation**: Explain the emotional or financial cost of *not* fixing it.
- **Personal Transition**: "I was there too, until I found..."

## 4. VALUE STACK
Build overwhelming value before revealing the price.
- **Tiers**: List features as outcomes. Tier 1 is the core, Tier 4 is a high-value bonus.
- **Total Value**: The sum of what everything is worth separately.
- **Your Price**: The significantly lower investment required today.

## 5. SOCIAL PROOF
Establish credibility through external validation.
- **3 Testimonials**: Must include specific results (e.g., "Increased revenue by 24% in 3 weeks").

## 6. TRANSFORMATION
Visualize the long-term journey.
- **Quick Win**: What happens in the first 24-48 hours.
- **Compound**: The benefits after 30 days of consistent use.
- **Advantage**: The competitive edge they gain.
- **10x**: The ultimate, long-term state of success.

## 7. SECONDARY CTA
Low-friction catch-all.
- **Avatar Stack**: Show a few users.
- **Question Headline**: "Ready to [Outcome]?"
- **"Yes" Button**: Action-oriented (e.g., "Yes, I want this").

## 8. FOOTER
Professionalism and navigation.
- Standard links: Privacy Policy, Terms, Contact, Socials.
