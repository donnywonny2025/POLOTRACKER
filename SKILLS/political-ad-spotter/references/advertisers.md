## YouTube Search Query Templates

```
# Find all ads from a specific org
"[EXACT ORG NAME]" campaign ad

# Find ads in a specific race
"[STATE]" "[OFFICE]" campaign ad 2026

# Find ads on a specific issue
"[ISSUE]" political ad 30 seconds 2026

# Find newest ads
political campaign ad 2026 newest
```

## Google Ads Transparency Center URL Patterns

Base URL: `https://adstransparency.google.com`

Search by advertiser: navigate to the URL and use the search bar.
Filter options: Platform (YouTube), Region (US), Political only, Date range.
