## Strategic Insight

**Most campaigns don't post "video editor wanted" jobs.** They either:
1. **Hire through agencies** (GMMB, BPI, SKDK)
2. **Use existing vendor relationships** (referrals)
3. **Hire freelancers directly** (via personal networks, not job boards)
4. **Post broader "digital" roles** that include video

**This means:** Job boards are just ONE data source. We need to:
- **Monitor campaign launches** (new campaigns = new vendor opportunities)
- **Track media director hires** (new media directors = potential new vendor relationships)
- **Research media buying agencies** (they control most video work)
- **Use LinkedIn** (more video-specific postings than GAIN Power)
- **Direct outreach** (most video work comes from relationships, not job postings)
