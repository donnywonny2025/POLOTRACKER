## KEY CONTACTS TO FIND

For each campaign/organization, we need to find:

1. **Campaign Manager** — overall decision-maker
2. **Communications Director** — manages messaging and media
3. **Digital Director** — manages online/social media (may oversee video)
4. **Media Buyer** — if they have one (may control video vendor relationships)
5. **Video Editor/Producer** — if they have in-house staff (potential collaborators)

**How to find:**
- LinkedIn search: "[Campaign Name]" + "[Title]"
- Campaign website "Team" or "About" pages
- FEC filings (staff salaries are public record)
- Political job boards (who's hiring = who's building teams)
- Twitter/social media (campaign staff often list their roles in bios)
